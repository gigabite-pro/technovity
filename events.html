Events



Tinkerathon

Participants have to design an innovative Arduino based project on the provided theme/ prompt using only a limited number of total unique components.

Hackathon

In a 4 day timeframe, participants will have to develop a product based on the given prompt and the qualified teams would then have to pitch/ present their product in the finals.


Dimensions

The event will proceed through 2 rounds(preliminary and finals), participants would be asked to 3D design on the basis of the given prompt.

SuperNova

Teams would be asked to research, innovate and prepare a 15-slide presentation and a video. Five qualifying teams will get to present it live for the finals in front of a panel of judges.

Xhibition

Students would present an innovative solution/ alternative to the problem provided.

TechCraft

Innovation+Minecraft. This two round event would ask the participants to use their minecraft skills to build, animate and present their project as a response to the prompt provided.
