Technovity

TEDx 2019 revolves around the theme why not which aims at bringing about a change in the conventional mindsets of the audience who ask themselves “Why” all the time, but fail to ask “Why not".

Humans possess the priceless gift of thought, logic, reason and rationality: something which makes us truly unique, and capable of shifting and transforming the world.

Our past is an evidence of rebellious change makers who refused to accept the traditional ways of the world and had the courage to question thereby bringing about a change, creating a present and future full of opportunities.

This year, we aim to harness these precious gifts and present our audience with compelling, and thought provoking questions.
