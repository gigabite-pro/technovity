Technovity

"You should not give up and we should not allow the problem to defeat us." ~ APJ Abdul Kalam

The mega event being organised by Amity International School Sec-46 aims to inspire innovation, creativity and critical thinking in students.

Proposing solutions to real world issues faced by people while competing against some of the best minds in NCR, the 8 sub events ranging from 3D designing to IoT and space Science, cover a wide range of technological skills.

We endeavour to bring out the best out of each participant via our enticing and perplexing tasks, to help them prepare for what the future might hold.

With that, we invite you to participate in the first ever Technovity interschool competition!
